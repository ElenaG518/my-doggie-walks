Dog Walking

All walks are private and may be scheduled for 30, 45, or 60 minutes. I can also accomodate longer or shorter walks. Contact me to discuss. If you are looking to get your dog some exercise, I also offer 20 minute runs. For longer exercise, I am also available to take your dog for a hike to one of our local mountains for a 2 hour hike.

Dog Hiking

Do you have a high-energy dog that is always ready to explore and requires a longer form of exercise and stimulation? I'll take your dog for a hike to Temescal Canyon or any other local trail of your choice. Dogs are required to and will be leashed at all times. Hikes normally take about two hours, plus commute time to and from your place. Commute time is included in the price within a 5 mile radius from Mar Vista.

Dog Running

Do you have a dog that requires more intensive exercise, or needs to burn off a little weight? Then you may consider a 20 minute run for your pooch. All runs can be customized by 5 minute increments or decrements.

Pet Sitting

I am available to come and pet sit and house sit while you are away. Rest assured that your pet will be in loving, caring hands while you are away. I watch over dogs, cats, bunnies, birds and small reptiles. Feel free to contact me if your pet falls outside of these categories to discuss.
